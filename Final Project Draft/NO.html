Setting for Conflict

The Northwest Ordinance was formed July 13, 1787. This act created the Northwest Territory, which was land that the British ceded to the United States in the Treaty of Paris. As the other pages can tell you, the attempts by the United States to settle the land was met with resistance by the Native Americans, along with the issues of money and the British Army continuing to fortify their own bases there. This is also the area where the Northwest Indian War took place, which would be quite an issue later on.
